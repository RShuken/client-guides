Understanding Rate Limits

Why Vishnu sometimes says "rate limit exceeded"

It's not a bug ‚Äî it's how the AI service manages traffic. Here's what's happening and how to fix it.

üîç What's actually happening behind the scenes

When you type a message ‚Äî even something simple like "test 123" ‚Äî Vishnu doesn't just send those 3 words to the AI. It packages up everything the AI needs to understand who it is and what you've been talking about:

System prompt ‚Äî ~15,000 tokens
Your files (SOUL, AGENTS, etc.) ‚Äî ~5,000 tokens
Chat history ‚Äî ~2,000+ tokens (grows)
Your message "test 123" ‚Äî 3 tokens
Total per message: ~22,000+ tokens

Think of it like calling a new assistant every time ‚Äî you have to re-explain who they are, what they're working on, and read back the entire conversation before asking your question. That's what those tokens are.

üìñ See the full visual explainer ‚Üí

‚ö†Ô∏è The rate limit problem

Anthropic (the company behind Claude, Vishnu's AI brain) limits how many tokens you can send per minute. On the starter tier, that limit is 30,000 tokens per minute.

Do the math:

üí¨ One message in Group Chat 1 ‚Üí ~25,000 tokens

üí¨ One message in Group Chat 2 ‚Üí ~25,000 tokens

Total: ~50,000 tokens ‚Äî already over the 30,000/min limit! üö´

This is why even two simple messages sent close together across different chats can trigger the error. It's not about how long your message is ‚Äî it's about the total context package hitting the API.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Vishnu always running?
Yes! Vishnu runs 24/7 on your Mac Mini. As long as your Mac Mini is powered on and connected to the internet, Vishnu is ready to chat. If you ever notice it's not responding, reach out to us at OpenClaw Install.
Can other people talk to Vishnu?
In DMs, only you can talk to Vishnu. In group chats, anyone in the group can interact with Vishnu. This is great for collaboration ‚Äî add team members to project groups and everyone can work with Vishnu together.
Does Vishnu remember previous conversations?
Yes! Vishnu has a built-in memory system. It writes important context to memory files that persist across sessions. Long-term memory survives restarts. If you use /reset, it only clears the current session ‚Äî long-term memory stays intact.

Can Vishnu work on multiple projects at once?
Absolutely. The best approach is to create separate Telegram groups for each project. Each group gets its own isolated memory, so contexts won't bleed between projects.
How do I start a new project?
Just describe what you want in plain English ‚Äî either in DMs or in a dedicated group chat. Vishnu will ask clarifying questions, build a requirements doc and plan, then start building. It follows a structured 8-step workflow to ensure quality.

Yes! You can send images (great for screenshots and visual reference), documents, voice messages, and other files through Telegram. Vishnu can analyze images, read documents, and process what you send.
